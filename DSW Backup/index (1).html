Online Will

When you die, a will specifies how your property, possessions, money, and other assets will be dispersed, giving you peace of mind that your estate will be handled properly. Our online will is just what you need if you need a basic and straightforward will.

Funeral Cover Payouts

Funeral cover is a type of insurance that pays a set amount of money in the case of a death, guaranteeing that the costs of a funeral are covered so that family members are not financially burdened during this terrible time.

Death Certificate Application

A death certificate is a legal document produced by a medical practitioner that states when a person died, or a document issued by a government civil registration office that proclaims the date, location, and cause of a person's death as recorded in an official death register.
